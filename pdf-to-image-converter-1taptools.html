Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF to Image converter really free?
Yes, completely free. 1taptools PDF to Image Converter is 100% free with no hidden costs, no account required, and no file limit restrictions.
Is my PDF file safe? Do you store my files?
Absolutely safe. Your PDF never leaves your device. All processing happens locally in your browser using PDF.js. We never upload, store, or access your files.
What image formats can I export to?
You can export to JPG (smaller file size, great for photos) or PNG (lossless quality, great for text-heavy documents). Choose your preferred format before converting.
Can I convert multi-page PDFs?
Yes! Every page of your PDF is converted to a separate image. You can download each page individually or download all pages at once as a ZIP archive.
What is the maximum PDF file size supported?
The tool supports PDF files up to 100MB. For very large PDFs with many pages, conversion may take a few extra seconds depending on your device speed.
Does this work on mobile phones?
Yes, the tool is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. Use Chrome or Safari on mobile for the best experience.
